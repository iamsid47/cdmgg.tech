What Exactly Is Codemugg?

Codemugg is a 501(c3) nonprofit organization registered in Kentucky, United States.

Our organization aims to provide education in programming (technology) for free. We achieve this goal by outsourcing teachers who are experienced in how to code but also know how to teach.

Our target audience is people who are beginners in programming or those who have not started yet. We would be providing courses on various topics covering programming languages, frameworks, and artificial intelligence. These topics would be taught in the form of courses (which would further be divided into sections.) After completing a course, one will be receiving a certificate for successfully completing the course.

The goal which we are trying to achieve is to make technical education available for free and for everyone. We mainly focus on Quality of learning and serving it in an APPROPRIATE amount.
